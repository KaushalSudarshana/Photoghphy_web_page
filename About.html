About

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on the North West coast of Scotland. His biggest passion in photography is for the natural world. He sells many images of the rugged Scottish landscape, its natural wildlife, and coastal birds. However, it’s not just landscape and wildlife photography he is interested in; like many photographers, he can also be hired for weddings, portraits, and special events.Malcolm's journey in photography began at a young age when he received his first camera from his father. Since then, he has honed his skills through years of practice and exploration of Scotland's diverse landscapes.His work has been featured in several publications and exhibitions, garnering praise for its ability to capture the raw beauty of Scotland.
